AI in business: practical applications and strategic choices

Updated: 21.2.2026

Summary: AI creates value when it solves clear business problems with measurable impact — not when it is deployed as a trend project.

Where value actually appears

  • Customer service workflow support
  • Operational automation of repetitive tasks
  • Data-supported forecasting and risk detection
  • Agent-driven research and drafting workflows

Strategic choices

  • Start with 1–2 high-friction process bottlenecks
  • Ensure data quality before scaling models
  • Define governance and responsible usage clearly
  • Measure outcomes in time saved and decision quality

Key takeaways

  • Focus on practical workflows, not AI theater.
  • Prioritization matters more than model novelty.
  • AI should strengthen execution and decision quality.
